Welcome to House Rent Portal

KTO will change the home buying experience with its search engine designed to deliver information that will save homebuyers significant time and money on their way to finding their dream home.

The KTO experience will engage users with a subscription based mobile application that will help guide users through the home buying process. KTO will educate users on the home buying process and will match them to programs offering free money at the Federal, State and Local Government levels. Customer outcomes will vary based upon individual circumstances (e.g. debt to income ratio, salary and income, assets, credit score and payment history, etc.).

KTO aims to match users based upon how they qualify for first-time home buying programs.
